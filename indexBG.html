Beginner Guitar

How to Learn Guitar

There are four main things to work on.

Technique

Guitar specific techniques. The things that involve muscle memory. Memorizing patterns like chord and scale shapes. Things that require repitions to play correctly and quickly.

Songs

There are two main reasons that people learn an instrument. To play other peoples songs, and to write their own songs. It's alot easier to start by learning other peoples songs. You will draw inspiration and learn about songwriting in the process.

Music Theory

Music Theory is the language musicians use to think about and discuss music. Even If you learn some songs and develop good technique if you don't take the time to learn a little theory you will struggle to keep up with other musicians and will not be able to learn as quickly. (Good ears can go a long way towards making up for this but we will get into that later.)
